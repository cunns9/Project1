About Us

Cunni's Pizza & Grill was statrted by three members of our family. We visioned a health food restaurant and started in 2013 with salads and natural juices. Eventually, we saw the need to add freshly homemade burgers and pizzas to our menu. Thus, we researched and find the best brand in meats to provide our customers with a healthy choice of their favourite meals. Today we are proud to serve you and the community.
